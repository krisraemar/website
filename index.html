WHAT WE DO

We develop digital biomarkers and algorithms using physiological data and wearable technology. Our key focus lies in creating solutions that improve exercise adherence, prevent injuries, and optimize training for middle-aged and elderly recreational and professional athletes.

We address the challenges of transforming physiological data into actionable insights, enabling personalized health and wellness solutions, accelerating scientific discovery, and enhancing performance.

OUR PROGRAMS

The Master Athlete

Current exercise recommendations are often based on observational studies or short-term interventions, which may not reflect the optimal training protocols for high-performance individuals. Our work aims to fill this gap by developing precise training load optimization strategies tailored for highly active older adults.

Learn More

Injury Prevention

By studying physiological data from active individuals, we develop strategies to maintain high physical function and performance in later life, as well as identify potential injury risk markers associated with sustained physical activity. Our goal is to prevent these injuries and ensure long-term athletic performance.

Learn More

Novel Digital Biomarkers

Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning are revolutionizing at-home data capture and analytics. By utilizing AI-based motion capture algorithms, we provide precise assessments of hip joint angles, mobility, and stability. This technology, combined with a targeted exercise program, aims to improve hip joint and muscle function and overall mobility.
